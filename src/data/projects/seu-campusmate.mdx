# SEU CampusMate

## Overview
SEU CampusMate is built specifically to help Southeast University students access essential campus services: attendance tracking, fee and waiver calculators, CGPA tools, club directories and campus news — all in one app.

## Key Features
- Attendance & CGPA calculators
- Tuition & waiver calculators
- Campus news, club listings & events
- Offline calculators and quick utilities
- Simple admin interface to publish campus announcements

## Tech Stack
- Android (Java/Kotlin)
- Backend: PHP + MySQL
- Firebase: analytics & notifications
- Design: Figma, Photoshop

## Impact
- Helps streamline daily student tasks and reduces manual calculation errors.
- Strengthened campus community engagement via club & events module.

## Future Plans
- Add authenticated student accounts and SSO integration with university portals.
